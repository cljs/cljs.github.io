bit-xor

function/macroAdded in 0.0-927 clojure.core/bit-xor
(bit-xor x y)
(bit-xor x y & more)

Details:

Bitwise "exclusive or". Same as x ^ y in JavaScript.

Examples:

Bits can be entered using radix notation:

(bit-xor 2r1100 2r1010)
;;=> 6
;; 6 = 2r0110

Same numbers in decimal:

(bit-xor 12 10)
;;=> 6

  ([x y & more]
