cljs.core/*

function/macroAdded in 0.0-927 clojure.core/*
  • (*)
  • (* x)
  • (* x y)
  • (* x y & more)

Returns the product of nums.

(*) returns 1.

Examples:

;; there is an implicit 1
(*)
;;=> 1

;; the implicit 1 comes into play
(* 6)
;;=> 6

(* 2 3)
;;=> 6

(* 2 3 4)
;;=> 24
